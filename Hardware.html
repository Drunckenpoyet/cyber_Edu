Course Overview

The essential hardware components that form the backbone of a network, primarily including high-performance routers and switches responsible for directing large volumes of data traffic across different network segments, ensuring efficient and reliable data transmission throughout the network.

Curriculum

  • Introduction to Computer Hardware
  • Storage Devices & Data Management
  • Peripheral Devices & Expansion
  • Networking Hardware & Connectivity
  • Embedded Systems & IoT Hardware
  • Modems, routers, switches, access points
  • Network Interface Cards (NICs) and adapters & HDD, SSD, NVMe, and external storage
  • Troubleshooting & Maintenance
