Course Overview

Networking is the process of connecting computers and devices to share resources, data, and services. It is the backbone of modern communication.

Curriculum

  • Introduction to Networking
  • Security Basics and Structure
  • Wireless & Cloud Networking – Connecting devices wirelessly and using cloud-based networks.
  • IP Addressing & Subnetting – Assigning unique IDs to devices for communication.
  • OSI (Open Systems Interconnection) model & TCP/IP Model (Internet Protocol Suite)
  • Routing & Switching – Directing data between networks and devices efficiently.
  • LAN, WAN, & VPN – Different types of networks for local/global communication.
